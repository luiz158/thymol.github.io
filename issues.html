As with all software, users of Thymol will encounter problems. Some problems are well known and ongoing, some will be new and easily remedied. Most fall somewhere in between.

You can review the well known and ongoing Thymol issues on the "known issues" page.

To formally report new issues or potential defects or to review progress on fixes please visit the issues reporting site
